Description

Large language models consume significant resources, leading to increased CO2 emissions. GPTgreen aims to spread awareness and provide tools to quantify and reduce the environmental impact of AI usage.

Awareness

We have developed tools to track AI-generated resource consumption:

  • Live Resource Consumption Chrome Extension - Tracks real-time energy, water, and CO2 usage while using AI.
  • Long-Term Resource Consumption Analytics - A website that aggregates long-term AI energy consumption data.

Reduction

Our Chrome Extension includes an "Energy Saver" mode, which automatically optimizes queries to minimize text generation and reduce environmental impact.

Research & Sources

We base our calculations on peer-reviewed research and provide sources on our website to ensure transparency.
